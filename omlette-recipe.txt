Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - salt
 - pepper
 - 1 onion


Directions:
 1. Cut the onion fine.
 
 2. Preheat a frying pan with oil for 1 min on low flame.
 
 3. Mix the 2 eggs with salt pepper and finely cut onion in the meanwhile.

 4. Pour the mixture on the pan and reduce the flame. Heat the pan while covered for 3 mins.

 5. Invert the thick omlette so that the upper portion gets fried as well and switch the flame off. Keep the pan open so that excess vapor evaporates.
